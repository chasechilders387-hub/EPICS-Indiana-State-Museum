Project Overview

Our EPICS team is collaborating with the Indiana State Museum to design and develop a new exhibit to enhance the visitor experience. The project allows us to apply our engineering and design principles to real-world community needs.

Goals

  • Identify a challenge faced by the museum or its visitors.
  • Design and prototype an innovative, sustainable solution.
  • Collaborate with museum staff to test and refine the idea.

Progress

We are currently making the exhibit, gathering materials and finding the right centerpiece for our Boilermaker exhibit.
