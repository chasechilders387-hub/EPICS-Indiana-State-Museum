Project Overview

Our EPICS team is collaborating with the Indiana State Museum to design and develop solutions that improve the visitor experience. The project allows us to apply engineering and design principles to real-world community needs.

Goals

  • Identify a challenge faced by the museum or its visitors.
  • Design and prototype an innovative, sustainable solution.
  • Collaborate with museum staff to test and refine the idea.

Progress

We are currently in the design phase, gathering requirements from museum representatives and researching possible engineering approaches. Updates will be added here as we progress through the semester.
