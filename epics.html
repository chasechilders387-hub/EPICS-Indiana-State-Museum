What is EPICS?

EPICS (Engineering Projects in Community Service) is a service-learning program at Purdue University in Indianapolis. Students work in multidisciplinary teams to design, build, and deliver engineering-based solutions for local community partners.

Learning Goals

  • Gain hands-on design and teamwork experience.
  • Apply classroom knowledge to community challenges.
  • Develop professional communication and leadership skills.

EPICS at Purdue Indianapolis

The Purdue Indianapolis EPICS program partners with organizations like the Indiana State Museum to provide students with real opportunities to make an impact in their community while learning the engineering design process.
